Welcome toDexter Foods

We are an India-based rice exporting company specializing in the sourcing, processing, and export of high-quality Indian rice to international markets. Our company operates with a clear objective: to deliver consistent quality, competitive pricing, and dependable service to our global partners.

Our Services

By working closely with rice mills, quality inspectors, and logistics providers, we ensure that each consignment is carefully selected, hygienically processed, and professionally packed. Our export operations are aligned with international trade standards, making us a reliable supplier for importers, wholesalers, and food distributors worldwide.

We believe in long-term business relationships, built on trust, transparency, and mutual growth.

Export-Standard Product Quality

Our rice is processed and packed in compliance with international export standards, ensuring consistency in grain quality, hygiene, and buyer specifications.

Timely Delivery Commitment

We plan shipments carefully and coordinate closely with logistics partners to ensure on-time dispatch and dependable delivery schedules.

Fast & Efficient Supply Chain

With strong links across mills, transporters, and ports, we maintain a fast-moving supply chain that minimizes delays and maximizes reliability.

Reliable Sourcing & Quality Team

Our experienced sourcing and inspection team ensures steady supply, quality control, and transparency at every stage of procurement.

About

Our focus is on Non-Basmati and Basmati rice varieties, sourced directly from reliable mills and farmers, processed under strict quality control, and packed to meet global food safety norms. With a strong logistics network and export experience, we ensure that every shipment meets customer expectations.
